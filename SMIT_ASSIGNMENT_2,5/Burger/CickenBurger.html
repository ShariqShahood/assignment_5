Chicken BURGER RECIPE

KFC - The Pioneers of Chicken Burger

Kentucky Fried chicken (KFC)was the first vendor to introduce chicken burger in the world. The original recipe of KFC chicken is the most authentic and globally demanded one. Later chicken was also offered by other restaurants but obviously the taste differs as compared to KFC chicken burgers.
KFC also offers other burgers which are KFC nacho burger, KFC tower burger, KFC bunless burger, KFC beef burger and many more.

Recipe of Chicken Burger by KFC

Chicken burger recipe is one of those fast foods which got popular within very short period of time. Chicken burger is actually product of a famous American Fast Food Company "KFC", however later it was offered by other local and multi- national food chains. Chicken burger, other than traditional burgers and bun kababs, made use of chicken instead of beef meat. Large pieces of chicken (usually chest pieces) are taken for the burger. These whole chicken pieces are then spiced and deep fried in the oil. The use of eggs on chicken also vivifies the taste of meaty filaments in the burger. The fried chicken becomes crispy and very tasty due to breadcrumbs and other condiments used with it.

Chicken Fillets Used in Chickens
