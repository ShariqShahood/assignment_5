KFC - The Pioneers of Beef Burger

Kentucky Fried beef (KFC)was the first vendor to introduce beef burger in the world. The original recipe of KFC beef is the most authentic and globally demanded one. Later beef was also offered by other restaurants but obviously the taste differs as compared to KFC beef burgers.
KFC also offers other burgers which are KFC nacho burger, KFC tower burger, KFC bunless burger, KFC beef burger and many more.

Recipe of Beef Burger by KFC

Beef burger recipe is one of those fast foods which got popular within very short period of time. Beef burger is actually product of a famous American Fast Food Company "KFC", however later it was offered by other local and multi- national food chains. Beef burger, other than traditional burgers and bun kababs, made use of beef instead of beef meat. Large pieces of beef (usually chest pieces) are taken for the burger. These whole beef pieces are then spiced and deep fried in the oil. The use of eggs on beef also vivifies the taste of meaty filaments in the burger. The fried beef becomes crispy and very tasty due to breadcrumbs and other condiments used with it.
