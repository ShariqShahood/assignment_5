MALAI TIKKA

A delicious malai chicken tikka recipe made with chicken chunks marinated in a creamy sauce. Bake this chicken tikka kebab recipe in the oven or grill it on the outdoor BBQ. These reshmi kebab starters will soon become your new favourite summer barbeque recipe.
A tender and juicy reshmi kebab recipe perfect served with homemade naan. This murgh malai tikka video shows you how to make chicken tikka that is juicy, moist, and so flavourful.
Chicken malai kebab is best served as an appetizer, but this chicken malai tikka recipe is also good for dinner parties and family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 700 g Chicken
  • 100 g Yoghurt
  • 100 g Double Cream Malai
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Garlic Paste
  • ▢1 tsp Ginger Paste
  • ▢1 tsp Green Chilli Paste
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Cornflour
  • 1 tsp Dried Fenugreek Leaves Methi
  • 1 tbsp Coriander

    • Do ahead:Mayonnaise can be made 6 hours ahead. Cover and chill.
